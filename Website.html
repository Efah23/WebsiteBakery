About Us

History: Established in 2021, The Frosted Whisk is a boutique home bakery in Pretoria. We specialise in custom cakes, gourmet cupcakes, and artisanal desserts, all made with love and high-quality ingredients.

Mission: To create magical and delicious moments by crafting beautiful, custom desserts that taste as wonderful as they look.

Vision: To be the first choice for memorable celebrations by spreading joy, one sprinkle at a time.

Target Audience: Parents, couples, and individuals looking for unforgettable treats for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and baby showers.
